🍰 New York Cheesecake

← Home

Description

Rich, creamy, and irresistibly smooth, New York cheesecake is a timeless dessert known for its dense texture and buttery biscuit crust. Every bite melts in your mouth with a perfect balance of sweetness and tanginess.

Whether served plain or topped with fresh berries, this classic dessert is perfect for birthdays, celebrations, or simply treating yourself.

Ingredients

  • 250 g digestive biscuits
  • 100 g melted butter
  • 600 g cream cheese
  • 200 g sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 100 ml heavy cream
  • Fresh berries (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 160°C.
  2. Crush the biscuits and mix with melted butter.
  3. Press the mixture into a springform pan.
  4. Beat cream cheese until smooth.
  5. Add sugar, eggs, vanilla, and heavy cream.
  6. Pour the filling over the crust.
  7. Bake for about 55 minutes.
  8. Allow to cool completely before refrigerating for at least 6 hours.
  9. Serve chilled with fresh berries.
